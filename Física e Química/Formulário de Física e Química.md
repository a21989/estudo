## FORMULÁRIO

### Quantidade, massa e volume

* $n = \frac{N}{N_A}$
* $M = \frac{m}{n}$
* $V_m = \frac{V}{n}$
* $\rho = \frac{m}{V}$

### Soluções

* $c = \frac{n}{V}$
* $x_A = \frac{n_A}{n_{\text{total}}}$
* $\text{pH} = - \log \{[\text{H}_3 \text{O}^+]/\text{mol dm}^{-3}\}$

### Energia

* $E_c = \frac{1}{2} \ m\ v^2$
* $E_{pg} = m \ g \ h$
* $E_m = E_c + E_p$
* $W = F \ d \ \cos \alpha$
* $\sum W = \Delta E_c$
* $W_{\vec{F_g}} = - \Delta E_{pg}$
* $U = R \ I$
* $P = R \ I^2$
* $U = \epsilon - r \ I$
* $E = m \ c \ \Delta T$
* $\Delta U = W + Q$
* $E_r = \frac{P}{A}$

### Mecânica

* $x = x_0 + v_0 \ t + \frac{1}{2} \ a \ t^2$
* $v = v_0 + a \ t$
* $a_c = \frac{v^2}{r}$
* $\omega = \frac{2 \pi}{T}$
* $v = \omega \ r$
* $\vec{F} = m \ \vec{a}$
* $F_g = G \ \frac{m_1 \ m_2}{r^2}$

### Ondas e eletromagnetismo

* $\lambda = \frac{v}{f}$
* $\Phi_m = B \ A \ \cos \alpha$
* $| \epsilon_i | = \frac{\Delta \ \Phi_m}{\Delta t}$
* $n = \frac{c}{v}$
* $n_1 \ \sin \alpha_1 = n_2 \ \sin \alpha_2$
